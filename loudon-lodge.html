About the Venue

A Unique Setting

Loudon Lodge is a charming building situated within Derby Arboretum, offering a truly unique setting for private events, meetings, community gatherings, and celebrations.

Named after John Claudius Loudon, the visionary landscape designer who created the Arboretum, the Lodge combines historic character with the tranquil beauty of its parkland surroundings.
