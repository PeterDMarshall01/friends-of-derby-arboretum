September 2021 · Community Festival

Lark in the Park

A joyful community festival in Derby Arboretum Park, centred around the fountain and community garden. The day brought together crafts, activities, games and information stalls alongside a packed programme of live music, singing, spoken word, yoga dance, a percussion workshop and a magician — something for everyone.
